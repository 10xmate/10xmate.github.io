10xmate4Slack Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 20th, 2020

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, disclosure and retention of information that we receive when you use 10xMate4Slack. By using the app, you agree to our terms and policies and consent to the collection, use, disclosure and retention of the information in accordance with this Policy. For the purposes of this document, we will refer to the 10xMate4Slack application as ‘the app’ or ‘this app’.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect the following information when you use the app to send a thank you card:

  • The name of your Slack workspace
  • The team value you select
  • The image you select
  • Whether you select a single or multiple receivers
  • How the command is triggered (using @ or not)

We use the information we receive through the use of this app to understand how the app is used and the number of companies that use it. We may also share aggregate, anonymous information, trends, and other analysis that we create based on the non-personally identifiable information we receive from you and other customers for marketing and advertising purposes.

We DO NOT collect, use, or disclose any of your personal data. Personal data is the information that can be used to identify a person. It may include, but is not limited to email address, first name and last name, cookies and usage data (e.g. your computer’s IP address, browser type, browser version, etc.). Also, we do not store the content of any of your messages.

2. Security & Storage

We use Heroku as a hosting provider in the United States to store the information we receive through the use of this app, in line with the latest security measures and standards. When you submit information via this app, it is protected and encrypted in transit though secure connections. We use industry standard encryption to protect your data (TLS/SSL). Since no transmission of data through Internet can be 100% secure, however, the transmission of information between you and our server is at your own risk.

3. Third Party Privacy Practices

This Privacy Policy addresses only the collection, use, disclosure and retention of information provided to us. For more details on the data disclosed to Slack while using this app, we encourage you to refer to Slack’s Privacy Policy.

4. Disclosure of Data

We may provide your non-personally identifiable information to other parties for marketing and advertising purposes.

5. Retention of Data

We use historical data to generate reports about the usage of the app. If you stop using the app, the data will still be available to us.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as needed, therefore, please review this page periodically for any changes. The revised Privacy Policy will be available on this page with an updated ‘Effective Date’ at the top of the page. You can accept the updated terms or stop using the app but your continued use of the app after the effectiveness of any update will be deemed to represent your consent to the provisions in the updated Privacy Policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us here: support@10xmate.com
